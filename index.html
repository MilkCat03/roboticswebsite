About Us

We are middle schoolers and high schoolers that go to Cannon JHS to practice robotics. Some high schools in Las Vegas do not have robotics teams; we allow those people to join our robotics team. The perfect example of this is our “mascot?” the Stay Puff Reaper, a sinister specter with a smile. We help the community with different outreach projects and we pride ourselves in keeping our word.

What we do outside of robotics

    We volunteer our time to help our home school Cannon Jr. High School by printing t-shirts out for the staff and for the sports teams. We also volunteer for a nonprofit organization called Homeless Teens for whom we designed a patented solar powered charger for them to use in their mobile center.
